Company Background:

Arawaza, founded in 1996, is a well-known brand in the martial arts equipment and apparel industry.
Headquartered in Canada, Arawaza aims to provide high-quality, affordable, and well-serviced products
for martial arts practitioners of all levels. They offer a wide range of products, including uniforms,
protective gear, and other accessories, including WKF-approved equipment.

History:

1996:
Arawaza was established with a focus on meeting the needs of martial arts practitioners.

Emphasis on Quality:
Arawaza is known for its commitment to quality, offering durable and long-lasting products.

WKF Approved:
Many Arawaza products, like their WKF-approved uniforms and protective gear, are used in official tournaments sanctioned by the World Karate Federation.

Global Presence:
Arawaza has expanded its reach with over 60 locations worldwide.
