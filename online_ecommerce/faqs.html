Frequently Asked Questions

What martial arts gear does Arawaza offer?

We provide top-quality equipment for Karate, Judo, Taekwondo, Kung Fu, Kobudo, and other martial arts disciplines.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, Arawaza ships worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary depending on your location.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number by email to monitor your shipment's progress.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods.

Can I return or exchange an item?

Yes. Returns or exchanges are accepted within 30 days of purchase, provided items are in original condition. Check our Return Policy for more details.

How do I contact customer service?

You can reach us via our Contact Page or email support@arawaza.com for assistance.
